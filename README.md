## Contact & Support
For help or questions email us at:
**support@codequest.app**

## Privacy Policy
CodeQuest collects only what's necessary:
- Email address (account creation)
- Username (chosen by you)  
- Learning progress (lessons, XP, streaks)

We never sell your data or use it for advertising.
You can delete your account anytime in Settings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is CodeQuest free?**
Yes, completely free.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Delete Account.

**What languages are available?**
Currently Python and C++. More coming soon.
